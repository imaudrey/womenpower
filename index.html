Welcome to WEquance!

Here at WEquance, we believe that equality is easily achievable. Excluding men from women empowerment, is not the best option, but instead, we know that men and women are just as equal. We created this news feed to help encourage other media sites to feature more females in the industry. By allowing females to have their spotlight, we hope that many can see how much they can provide to the world of media. We hope you enjoy WEquance news feed and hope you (man and woman alike) will enjoy this new representation of women.

Sports

Men’s sports are frequently featured in the media.However, women’s sports are not as frequently viewed as men’s even though they are just as talented, athletic, strong, etc. If not more! WE shed light on these female athletes here at WEquance and give them recognition for their immense amount of talent.

Fashion

Women of different body types are beautiful, empowering, amazing, and above all...unique! Everyone is flawless and WE want to show women that by providing them different resources for easier shopping. WE classified women into five categories: curvy, petite, tall, hourglass, and plus-size

Education

Women are portrayed in the working field, although not in significant numbers compared to men. In order to encourage people to feature women in the different educational subjects and fields, WE implemented information to help them see the light of women’s actions.
